Security Policy

Last updated: 26 October 2025 09:29 AM EDT

Data Collection

InferencePort AI is designed with privacy in mind, however, we require users to create accounts to ensure that our app is not abused


We collect the following information at signup:

  • Email (For user verification purposes)
  • Password (These are encrypted obviously)

      • Additionally, users will receive a default username such as user_a831b. Users may change this, however this will not be encrypted and public facing, so use at your own risk

      Open Source Transparency

      Our codebase is fully open source and available on GitHub. This ensures transparency and allows the community to audit, contribute to, and verify the security of the application.

      Third-Party Services

      InferencePort AI does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services EXCEPT Huggingface Spaces and user controlled websites. We do our best not transmit user data.

      Please also read the security and privacy policies of Huggingface and any websites that are added to the app for a complete view.

      Security Practices

      • All code is reviewed and tested before release.
      • Dependencies are regularly updated to patch known vulnerabilities.
      • We encourage responsible disclosure of any security issues via email.

      Contact

      If you have questions or concerns about security, please email me at sharktide@bugcrowdninja.com.

      We reserve the right to update and change this security / privacy policy at any time.
