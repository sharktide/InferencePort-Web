Security & Privacy Policy

Security & Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 February 2026

Account Requirements

InferencePort AI does not require users to create an account. The application can be used without signing in. Signing in is optional and is only required if you choose to enable certain features such as chat syncing or server connecting.

Chat Data & Syncing

By default, all chats are stored locally on your device. When you are not signed in, no chat data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.

If you sign in and explicitly enable chat syncing, your chat messages and conversation metadata are collected and stored securely to synchronize your chats across devices.

  • Chat syncing is disabled by default
  • Chats are only collected when both signed in AND syncing is enabled
  • You may disable chat syncing at any time, but this may not delete existing data
  • To request deletion of your chats, email [email protected]
☁ Lightning / Cloud Models: Requests to InferencePort Lightning — including chat messages and sessions — may be processed on our or our vendors' servers. We aim for zero-data retention but cannot guarantee it. Please read the privacy policies of Pollinations.AI, Groq, and Cerebras AI before using cloud features.

Data Collected When Signing In

If you choose to create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address (for authentication and account recovery)
  • Name
  • Encrypted authentication credentials
  • Optional username (public-facing, not encrypted)
  • Other data collected by third-party authentication providers

Do not use personally identifying information in your username.

Third-Party Services

InferencePort AI does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services by default. The application may interact with:

  • Hugging Face Spaces
  • User-configured or user-added websites
  • Lightning AI models may optionally use: Pollinations.ai, Groq Cloud, Cerebras

Data transmitted to these services is always user-initiated. Some services may process data on servers outside the United States.

Security Practices

  • All code is reviewed and tested before release
  • Dependencies are regularly updated to address vulnerabilities
  • Sensitive data is encrypted where applicable
  • InferencePort is fully open source — independently auditable on GitHub

Contact
