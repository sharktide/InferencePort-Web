Security & Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 December 2025

Account Requirements

InferencePort AI does not require users to create an account. The application can be fully used without signing in.

Signing in is optional and is only required if you choose to enable chat syncing across devices or other cloud-backed features.

Chat Data & Syncing

By default, all chats are stored locally on your device. When you are not signed in, no chat data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.

If you sign in and explicitly enable chat syncing, your chat messages and conversation metadata are collected and stored securely in order to synchronize your chats across devices.

  • Chat syncing is disabled by default
  • Chats are only collected when both signed in AND syncing is enabled
  • You may disable chat syncing at any time

Data Collected When Signing In

If you choose to create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address (for authentication and account recovery)
  • Encrypted authentication credentials
  • Optional username (public-facing, not encrypted)

Usernames are optional and visible to others where applicable. Do not use personally identifying information in your username.

Open Source Transparency

InferencePort AI is fully open source and available on GitHub. This allows independent auditing, verification, and community contribution.

Third-Party Services

InferencePort AI does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The application may interact with:

  • Hugging Face Spaces
  • User-configured or user-added websites

Data transmitted to these services is user-initiated. Please review their respective privacy policies.

Security Practices

  • All code is reviewed and tested before release
  • Dependencies are regularly updated to address vulnerabilities
  • Sensitive data is encrypted where applicable
  • Responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities is encouraged via email to the support email
