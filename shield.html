Architecture

4 layers. 8 pipeline stages.

Every request flows through four architectural layers, passing through eight analysis stages. Each contributes evidence.

📥
Layer 1: Signal Collection
Accept any available information — email, username, phone, IP, device fingerprint, session metadata, request content, historical data, custom fields. Flexible schema handles structured JSON or plain text. Shield normalizes everything.
🔍
Layer 2: Intelligence Engines
Email Intelligence detects disposable providers and suspicious domains. IP Intelligence finds VPNs, datacenter IPs, and abuse networks. Device Intelligence spots reuse and automation. Phone Intelligence flags VOIP and temporary numbers. Behavioral Intelligence catches velocity anomalies.
🧠
Layer 3: AI Reasoning Engine
Specialized AI models analyze all signals simultaneously. Traditional systems evaluate signals independently — Shield evaluates context. Email alone = low risk. Phone alone = low risk. IP alone = low risk. Combined pattern = high confidence abuse. The AI reasons across all available evidence.
⚖️
Layer 4: Decision Engine
Produces the final risk score, confidence score, decision, detailed explanation, and recommended action. Supports allow, challenge, rate limit, review, and block. Every decision is auditable with full evidence trail.

Verdicts

5 decisions. Zero ambiguity.

Shield doesn't hand you a number and walk away. It tells you exactly what to do next.

allow
Request is safe. Proceed without any intervention. Zero friction for legitimate users.
challenge
Suspicious activity. Present a CAPTCHA, send a verification email, or require 2FA.
rate_limit
Exceeds safe thresholds. Throttle requests, add cooldowns, or cap usage.
review
Needs a human look. Flag for manual inspection before allowing or blocking.
block
Malicious. Deny entirely. Block the IP, ban the account, kill the session.
